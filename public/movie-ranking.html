My top 3 movies of all-time

Harry Potter

No explanation needed I guess :D, all time favorite series that I can watch again and again

Abiyum Naanum - Tamil

Wonderful movie that depicts the relationship between father and daughter, through various stages that a girl baby goes through in her life.

Pirivom Sandhipom - Tamil

Family oriented subject, where a girl who has grown only with her parents, moves into a very big family after marriage. As she starts enjoying the nuances in that big household, her husband gets an internal transfer, and is subject to move to a different city. The rest of the story revolves around whether the girl adapts to this new shocking situation or not.
